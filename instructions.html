Welcome to Casa Italiana. Here you can practice learning Italian phrases as well as doing focus drills on lists of nouns and verbs using our flashcard interface. To begin, enter your name in the input box. Then choose whether you would like to start learning phrases (hint: click the LEARN button), or to just start testing your knowledge of Italian words (TEST button). Then just hit START, to begin and NEXT, to proceed to each new prompt. Hit STOP, to stop the game. Have Fun!
